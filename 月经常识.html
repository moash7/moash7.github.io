月经来几天是正常的？

How many days is normal for menstruation?

月经来几天因人而异，通常持续3至7天为正常范围。如果你的月经周期在这个范围内，那就是正常的。

The duration of menstruation varies from person to person, typically lasting between 3 to 7 days. If your menstrual cycle falls within this range, it's considered normal.

隔多久来月经是正常的？

How often is it normal to have a menstrual period?

正常的月经周期通常在21至35天之间，计算方式是从一个月经周期的第一天到下一个月经周期的第一天。如果你的月经周期在这个范围内，那就是正常的。

A normal menstrual cycle usually falls between 21 to 35 days, calculated from the first day of one period to the first day of the next. If your menstrual cycle falls within this range, it's considered normal.

卫生巾最好多久换一次？

How often should sanitary pads be changed?

卫生巾建议每2至4小时更换一次，以保持清洁和舒适。在月经第一天和流量较多的时候，可能需要更频繁地更换卫生巾。

It's recommended to change sanitary pads every 2 to 4 hours to maintain cleanliness and comfort. On the first day of menstruation and during heavy flow, you may need to change pads more frequently.

痛经能不能吃止痛药？

Can you take painkillers for menstrual cramps?

可以的，如果你感到痛经不适，可以使用一些非处方止痛药来缓解疼痛。但要注意按照正确的剂量使用，并遵循药品说明。

Yes, if you experience discomfort from menstrual cramps, you can take over-the-counter pain relievers to alleviate the pain. However, it's important to use them in the correct dosage and follow the instructions on the medication.

月经期间能不能吃冰？

Is it okay to have something cold during menstruation?

一般来说，月经期间可以吃冰，但对于某些人来说，冷食可能会加重痛经或增加月经不适感。如果你发现吃冷食会导致不适，最好避免食用。

Generally, it's okay to eat something cold during menstruation. However, for some individuals, cold foods may exacerbate menstrual cramps or increase discomfort. If you find that consuming cold foods causes discomfort, it's best to avoid them.

红糖水对痛经有没有用？

Does brown sugar water help with menstrual cramps?

有些人认为喝红糖水可以缓解痛经，但科学证据并不确定。如果你想尝试，可以适量饮用，但如果疼痛严重或持续，请咨询医生。

Some people believe that drinking brown sugar water can alleviate menstrual cramps, but scientific evidence is not conclusive. If you want to try it, you can drink it in moderation, but if the pain is severe or persistent, consult a doctor.

处女也可以使用棉条吗？

Can virgins use tampons?

可以的，处女也可以使用棉条。使用棉条不会损伤处女膜，但对于第一次使用者来说，可能需要一些练习和调整。选择适合你身体大小和流量的棉条，并确保正确使用。

Yes, virgins can also use tampons. Using tampons does not damage the hymen, but for first-time users, it may require some practice and adjustment. Choose tampons that are suitable for your body size and flow, and make sure to use them correctly.
